History of the Internet: The Dot.com Bubble

Amazon

Jeff Bezos' led Amazon in an innovative bussiness model that relied growth through debt financing instead of traditional revenue-led growth models. This change represented a significant advantage for digital companies as they had far more accessibility compared to traditional brick and mortar based store which were traditionally limited to serving only people in close geographical proximity.

Youtube

Chad Hurley created youtube, a video streaming platform which revolutionized digital content by allowing users to upload and edit digital videos from home, thereby changing everyday people from simply being consumers of content, transforming them into producers of content themselves. A process which completely flipped the traditional entertainment production model upside down. This inversion was made possible primarily by breakthroughs in end-to-end encryption which allowed users to safely purchase and store bank information online without fear of identity theft and helped usher in the new era of the digital economy.
